About FADO JEWELRY

Rooted in Lisbon, Worn Around the World

FADO JEWELRY was born in Lisbon from a simple observation: the most meaningful pieces people wore were never the most extravagant, but the ones with stories behind them. Our founders, raised among the tiled streets, Fado music, and historic goldsmiths of Portugal, set out to translate that quiet emotional resonance into contemporary jewelry. They began by partnering with master artisans in Porto and Lisbon, combining generational techniques with minimalist silhouettes. What started as a small collection for close friends quickly grew into an international brand, sought after by collectors who value authenticity and craft over fast fashion.

Our mission is to preserve and elevate Portuguese craftsmanship while creating jewelry that feels deeply personal, not seasonal. We are guided by three core values: integrity in materials, respect for the artisans behind every piece, and a commitment to timeless design that brings quiet confidence to the wearer. We envision a world where people buy fewer, better things—objects that carry memory, meaning, and place. With every FADO piece, we aim to offer more than jewelry: we offer a tangible connection to heritage, made to be worn every day and cherished for generations.
